## Harness Enrichment — cold-email
Pattern captured for the skill: **"200x arbitrage opener"** — when a brand's earned posts outperform owned by two orders of magnitude, the creator outreach email/DM should open with the creator's own number, not the brand's pitch ("Your post did 124,937 views — our median is 655"). Radical asymmetry disclosure builds instant credibility and frames the collab as the brand needing the creator, which raises reply rates for barter offers. Also: in Egypt D2C, the first collab touch is an IG DM in masri, not email — email is the follow-up/contract channel.

## Harness Enrichment — copywriting
Two reusable Egypt-D2C copy laws confirmed by this run's data: (1) **masri-first for feed, English for identity** — the brand's only above-median owned post is a masri skit while its 16 English captions flatline [S08]; (2) **"check-at-door" as headline, not footnote** — COD check-on-delivery policies buried in policy pages are the strongest trust copy available in COD markets and should be promoted to hook status (angle A8 template added to the swipe file).

## Harness Enrichment — ad-creative
Creative-testing seed matrix derived from evidence: axis 1 language (masri vs EN — expect masri to win, باشا مصر precedent), axis 2 creative source (creator whitelisted vs studio owned — expect creator, 200x organic gap), axis 3 message (trust/COD-check vs offer/sale-alert vs new-drop). Prediction to validate in paid: creator × masri × offer is the top cell. Whitelisting/spark-ads from creator handles (A2) added as the default amplification mechanic when owned-page ER is under 0.05%.

## Harness Enrichment — marketing-psychology
Principle bank additions: **risk-reversal is the dominant lever in COD cultures** (the courier-waits check ritual is a physical free-trial — name it in copy); **dormant-base reactivation before acquisition** (88.9K followers at 0.006% ER is a warm asset, cheaper than cold reach); **seasonal anchoring** (Ramadan tags in the catalog prove the purchase calendar — reactivation waves should be timed to it, not spread evenly).

Therefore: **the budget buys distribution for creator/masri content and builds the WhatsApp conversion bridge — it does not amplify the current owned content, which the evidence says nobody wants.**

### 3. Sequencing gates (order matters more than the split)
- **G0 — Language flip (week 0, $0):** masri-first captions on IG/TikTok/FB. Evidence: 16/17 English-only owned captions [S08] vs Arabic outperformance on TikTok [S09] + Egypt context law. No spend until this ships.
- **G1 — Mobile landing gate:** PageSpeed mobile = 55 [S06, HELD]. Do NOT scale paid traffic to mobile product pages until remediated OR route CTWA/Shop-native paths that bypass the slow landing. Desktop 98 is fine but Egyptian social traffic is mobile.
- **G2 — WhatsApp infra before CTWA spend:** a business WhatsApp with response ownership (Era 13 Q4) must exist before the 15% CTWA lane activates.
- **G3 — Creator contracts:** whitelist the proven earned creators (URLs + metrics on file [S08]) including founder-as-face `awadlolwa` [S11, LIKELY founder; S08 HELD posts] before buying cold reach.
- **G4 — Seasonal timing:** catalog proves Ramadan is the demand peak (largest collections in the store [S04][S05]); Ramadan 2027 lands ~Feb 2027 — the turnaround must be conversion-ready by Jan 2027. Eid and Black Friday are secondary spikes [S04 tags].

## Harness Enrichment — value-prop-canvas-fast

Evidence-backed prop candidates the canvas should test: (a) *try-before-you-pay* — check-on-delivery + in-house courier flexibility [S03 HELD] is a real functional differentiator that is currently buried in a policy page; (b) *Egyptian-made, European-material* provenance [S02 SELF_REPORTED]; (c) *fruit-leather sustainability* [HYPOTHESIS, needs client gate]. Pain relieved: fit/quality risk of online shoe buying in a COD market. Canvas enrichment: "check while the courier waits" is the strongest unclaimed message-market fit found in this run.

## Harness Enrichment — market-sizing
Egypt footwear ≈ EGP 31.4bn retail 2025, +9% YoY [S11 ESTIMATE_ONLY — conflicts with ~$635M USD-terms source; band, never settle]; women's ≈ 52%; offline ≈ 90% [S11 LIKELY]. The opportunity math for O1-O6 does NOT depend on resolving the band: with e-commerce at ~10% of a women's segment worth roughly EGP 16bn (banded), a single-digit-basis-point online share shift dwarfs any realistic Cielito revenue base. Sizing enrichment: express all opportunity value as share-of-online-women's-footwear movements, not absolute EGP, until client revenue data lands (GAP).

## Harness Enrichment — pricing-strategy
Band structure is healthy at core: 88 SKUs under EGP 1,000, 156 in 1,000-2,500, only 6 above 2,500 [S04 HELD] — a coherent accessible-premium ladder with rare halo items (EGP 7,600 vest). Issues: 38% permanent-feeling discount depth (-20% typical) erodes the anchor [S04 HELD]; inverted compare_at values on ≥3 corset SKUs [S05 HELD] actively break trust math. Enrichment: fix inverted anchors (hours of work), cap visible sale share, and let Ramadan/Eid (O5) carry promo weight seasonally instead of year-round.

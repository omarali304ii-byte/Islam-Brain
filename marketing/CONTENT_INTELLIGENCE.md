## Creative autopsy

- **Hook taxonomy that wins:** (1) named-character workplace skit ("تسنيم" series) · (2) short masri cultural nods ("باشا مصر") · (3) service/situational comedy. **What loses:** English "shop our new [product]" captions — the entire owned median.
- **Format × engagement (owned IG, n=17):** Video 10 · Sidecar 6 · Image 1. Reels instinct is right; the *content inside* them is the failure, not the format.
- **Language × engagement:** the clearest lever in the whole dataset — Arabic posts top both platforms; 16/17 owned captions are English. This is a free reach uplift.
- **Owned-vs-earned split:** earned content out-reaches owned ~190× on views and ~150× on likes at the top. The brand is sitting on a flywheel it doesn't operate.
- **Duration:** GAP — not captured (Apify TikTok payload didn't include reliable per-video duration in this pull; a data-pass route if needed).

## So-what → brief
Move the winning earned formats in-house, flip to masri, franchise the "تسنيم" series as an ownable engine, and repost creators (with permission) into the owned grid. Measured on NEW-format posts only, target 10× the owned median in 8 weeks (metrics_watchlist).

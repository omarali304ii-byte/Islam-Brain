# Brand Framework — Cielito Egypt (2026-07-09)
> Client-safe. Built from captured evidence. Provisional visual specifics flagged pending image review.

## Brand story
Cielito began (~2021) with founder **Lolwa Awad**'s frustration that every shoe looked the same and none carried her from a morning meeting to an evening event. The answer was occasion-flexible, design-led footwear — handmade in Egypt with internationally-sourced materials — sold at a price real women could pay. The brand's most distinctive early chapter was a sustainable **fruit-leather** innovation (aloe vera, prickly pear, pineapple) that earned unpaid national press — a differentiator now absent from every live surface and awaiting a founder decision on revival.

## Message house
**Roof (brand idea):** *One shoe. Every moment. Made in Egypt.*

| Pillar | Claim | Proof point (evidence) | Status today |
|---|---|---|---|
| Craft | Handmade in Egypt, real leather, built to last | About page; nappa-lining/leather catalog [S02/S04] | **Under-communicated** — customers praise looks, not craft [VoC] |
| Versatility | Morning-to-soirée, occasion-flexible | Founder origin insight [S11]; occasion catalog breadth [S04] | Latent — not a stated theme |
| Accessible luxury | Design-led without a designer price | EGP 1,200 median, 88 SKUs under 1K [S04] | Muddied by 38% discounting |
| Innovation (dormant) | First Egyptian fruit-leather brand | Fustany + Identity Mag [S11] | **Deleted** — founder-gated revival (D-05) |

## Voice
- **Current:** English-first, product-descriptive, inconsistent (three taglines). Reads distant in a masri market.
- **Recommended:** masri Egyptian Arabic first, warm, witty (the "تسنيم" skit voice is the brand's real, winning voice — it just isn't recognized as the brand voice yet), with concise English for premium capsules. Craft-confident, never discount-desperate.

## Tagline decision (3 competing today — must pick ONE)
- Current live: "Moment of Style" (site title) · "Timeless Designs" (homepage) · "Effortless style, premium quality" (bio) [S02/S10].
- Territory options: (a) *One shoe. Every moment.* (versatility — ownable, ties to origin story) · (b) *Made by Egyptian hands.* (craft/local pride — ties to top masri performers) · (c) *Timeless, not trend.* (design-led vs Dejavu volume). **Recommendation: (a)** as roof, (b) as craft signature line.

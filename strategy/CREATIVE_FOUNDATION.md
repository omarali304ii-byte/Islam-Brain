# Creative Foundation — Cielito Egypt (2026-07-09)
> Follows CREATIVE_FOUNDATION_PROTOCOL (10 sections). ⚠ Visual DNA marked PROVISIONAL — derived from copy/catalog/format evidence, NOT from pixel inspection (no image review this run). Lock against real assets before production.

## 1. Brand Analysis + Strategic Wedge
Cielito = handmade Egyptian occasion footwear + apparel, mid-price, real audience (89K IG) with a broken owned engine. **Strategic wedge:** the ~190× owned-vs-earned gap + the masri/English language mismatch = a fast, low-spend turnaround because the demand assets already exist. The differentiator wedge is the dormant fruit-leather story (only rival-uncontested claim).

## 2. Customer Psychology
Aspirational-but-value-conscious Egyptian women (25–40) under purchasing-power pressure. They buy beauty first (verbatims are aesthetic praise), trust real people over brands (UGC ~190× owned), shop mobile, and close via chat/COD. They want to feel stylish without overspending, and to back something local and authentic. Fear: overpaying / looking like everyone else / online sizing risk (answered by check-on-delivery).

## 3. Competitor Visual Analysis
Captured: Dejavu = high-frequency, trend-led, high-saturation mass look (289K IG) [S11]. Premium-craft rivals (Jayda Hany, Bulga, Ramla) = editorial, muted, heritage. **Gap:** warm, design-led, accessible-luxury with a sustainability edge is unoccupied at mid-price. ⚠ Rival visual teardown (actual grids) = data-pass route, not done this run.

## 4. Visual Research
⚠ Not conducted at pixel level. What's inferable: catalog has consistent ~4–5 images/SKU (a shoot routine exists); UGC-native phone-shot content out-performs — authenticity beats gloss for this audience.

## 5. Visual DNA (PROVISIONAL)
- **Palette:** terracotta/tan (leather + Egyptian earth) · soft cream (approachable premium) · deep oxblood/charcoal accent. Warm, not neon. (Lock vs assets.)
- **Light:** warm natural daylight; golden-hour for occasion; hard studio only for hero product.
- **Texture:** real leather grain, Egyptian-hands macro, phone-authentic UGC.
- **Mood:** confident, warm, effortless — "affordable luxury," never "sale rack."

# Cielito Egypt — Executive Brief
*Prepared for leadership · July 2026 · client-safe*

## The verdict in one line
You have a strong brand and a real audience separated by a broken content engine — and fixing it is fast and cheap because the assets already exist.

## The three facts that matter
1. **A large audience you can't reach.** 88,903 Instagram followers, verified — but recent posts earn a median of **3 likes**. Meanwhile, content that customers and creators post *about* Cielito reaches **5,930 likes and 124,937 views** — roughly **190× your own posts**. The demand is there; your own channel isn't capturing it.
2. **You're speaking the wrong language.** Your best-performing posts on both Instagram and TikTok are in Egyptian Arabic — yet 16 of your last 17 captions were in English. This is a free reach uplift you're leaving on the table.
3. **There's no way to buy from you where it counts.** There is no WhatsApp ordering path on your site, bio, or posts — in a market where WhatsApp is how people actually buy, and where your own "check before you pay" delivery already works like a conversation.

## The opportunity
This is not "build an audience from scratch" (slow, expensive). It's "repair the engine in front of an audience and a proven word-of-mouth flywheel you already have" (executable in 90 days, mostly without ad spend).

## The three decisions, in order
1. **Install WhatsApp ordering everywhere** — days, near-zero cost.
2. **Clean the catalog and switch to Arabic-first content** — two weeks.
3. **Turn your creators into a system** — the content that already outperforms yours, made repeatable with usage rights.
*(Then: fix mobile site speed, and make the founder-level call on reviving your press-famous fruit-leather line.)*

## What we still need from you
Order and traffic data (Shopify + analytics) so every "to be baselined" number in the dashboard becomes a real ROI projection — and one 60-minute founder conversation about the fruit-leather story and the tagline.

## What you're looking at next
A live **Cielito 360 command center** — the owned-vs-earned engine, your content leaderboard, catalog, site health, competitive picture, and a 90-day plan, all in one place, every number traceable to its source.

About Us
Welcome to Cielito
A brand That Will Make Every Moment,
A Moment of Style
At Cielito, we are dedicated to providing you with top-notch quality and unmatchable styles. Crafted by Egyptian hands with internationally sourced materials and accessories — from Italy, Spain, Germany, India, UK, and more — Cielito is creating an iconic identity in the footwear market.
With a range of genuine leather heels, sandals, and mules, made with nappa lining, and international-standard heels made in Egypt, Cielito offers leather lovers with a product that they will love and enjoy. With a focus on top finish, durability, and design, your genuine leather Cielito is sure to be a favourite in your show collection, and a pair that you can always rely on.
For vegans leather lovers, we have a variety of heels, sandals, and mules, from a wide palette of colours, and voguish designs that are sure to impress. With comfortable insoles, and durable vegan leather, we know you’ll love your Cielito for a long time.
